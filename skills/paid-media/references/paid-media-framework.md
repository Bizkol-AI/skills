# Paid Media Strategy Framework

Paid advertising strategy, budget distribution, creative analysis, and platform playbooks. Used by `/brand-audit` and informs paid media sections of reports.

## Budget Distribution Framework

Allocate based on client maturity, not fixed percentages:

**New clients (< 6 months):**
Weight toward high-intent, fast-feedback channels:
- 40% Google Search/Shopping (capture existing demand)
- 35% Meta Ads (demand generation)
- 15% Retargeting (across platforms)
- 10% Testing (new platforms, new creative angles)

**Mature clients (6+ months with data):**
Shift toward retention and brand building:
- 30% Acquisition (Google Search + Meta prospecting)
- 25% Retargeting/remarketing
- 25% Retention (email, loyalty, repeat customer campaigns)
- 10% Brand building (YouTube, TikTok upper funnel)
- 10% Testing

## Platform Playbooks

### Google Search Ads
- **Best for:** Capturing existing demand, high purchase intent
- **Key metrics:** CPA, ROAS, impression share, quality score
- **Strategy:** Start with exact match on highest-intent keywords, expand to phrase match as data accumulates
- **E-commerce specific:** Shopping campaigns are essential — optimize product feed, use Performance Max with caution

### Google Shopping
- **Best for:** E-commerce with strong visual products
- **Key metrics:** ROAS, click share, impression share
- **Strategy:** Product feed optimization is 80% of the work — titles, descriptions, images, pricing

### Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)
- **Best for:** Demand generation, audience building, retargeting
- **Key metrics:** CPM, CTR, CPA, ROAS, frequency
- **Strategy:** Creative is the targeting. Test broad audiences with strong creative before narrowing
- **Creative mix:** Test UGC vs branded, video vs static, problem-aware vs solution-aware

### TikTok Ads
- **Best for:** Younger demographics, products with visual/demonstration angles
- **Key metrics:** CPM, CTR, CPA, video completion rate
- **Strategy:** Native-feeling content outperforms polished ads. Use creator-style content.

### Pinterest Ads
- **Best for:** Lifestyle, home, fashion, food e-commerce
- **Key metrics:** CPM, outbound CTR, saves, CPA
- **Strategy:** Long content shelf life — pins continue driving traffic months after posting

## Creative Strategy

### Content Types by Funnel Stage

**Top of funnel (awareness):**
- Problem-aware hooks ("Tired of [problem]?")
- Curiosity/pattern interrupt
- Educational/value-first content
- Trending format adaptations

**Middle of funnel (consideration):**
- Product demonstrations
- Before/after transformations
- Comparison content (vs alternatives)
- Social proof / testimonials
- UGC reviews

**Bottom of funnel (conversion):**
- Offer-driven (discount, free shipping, guarantee)
- Urgency/scarcity (limited time, low stock)
- Retargeting with specific products viewed
- Customer story / case study

### UGC Integration Pipeline

1. Identify top-performing organic content (client's and competitors')
2. Repurpose winning formats into paid creative briefs
3. Source UGC creators or use customer-submitted content
4. A/B test UGC against branded creative
5. Feed winning UGC formats back to creators for more variations
6. UGC typically outperforms polished brand creative in CPM and CTR

### Creative Fatigue Signals
Watch for these week-over-week:
- CTR declining > 15%
- CPM rising > 20%
- Frequency exceeding 3.0
- Conversion rate dropping while spend is stable

When fatigue detected: refresh creative, adjust audience targeting, or pause and replace.

### Platform-Native Creative Guidelines

| Platform | Format | Aspect | Length | Style |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| Meta Feed | Image/Carousel | 1:1 or 4:5 | N/A | Thumb-stopping visual, clear value prop |
| Meta Stories/Reels | Video | 9:16 | 15-30s | Fast-paced, text overlays, hook in 3s |
| Google Search | Text | N/A | 30/90 char | Keyword-rich, benefit-led, strong CTA |
| Google Shopping | Product image | 1:1 | N/A | Clean product shot, white background |
| TikTok | Video | 9:16 | 15-60s | Native/raw feel, creator-style, trending audio |
| Pinterest | Image/Video | 2:3 | N/A | Aspirational, lifestyle context, text overlay |

## Ad Account Audit Checklist

When auditing existing ad accounts:

- [ ] Conversion tracking verified and firing correctly
- [ ] Attribution window appropriate for the business
- [ ] Campaign structure follows best practices (not too granular, not too broad)
- [ ] Budget allocation matches funnel priorities
- [ ] Negative keywords in place (Google)
- [ ] Audience exclusions set (Meta — exclude existing customers from prospecting)
- [ ] Creative variety (minimum 3-5 active creatives per ad set)
- [ ] No creative fatigue signals on active ads
- [ ] Landing page experience matches ad promise
- [ ] Retargeting campaigns active with appropriate windows
